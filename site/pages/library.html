Library

We have a HUEG library of anime and manga which all members are free to borrow from. If you wish to borrow anything from the library then be sure to turn up at a Tuesday showings (unless stated otherwise) and you'll be able to borrow things during the break.

We generally ask for a £5 deposit per item, which is of course returned to you when you return the item; and we expect the item back after a period of 2 weeks (unless you decide to renew it).
